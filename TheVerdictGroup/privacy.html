Privacy Policy

The Verdict Group is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website or engage our legal services.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and other contact information when you submit a contact form or request a consultation through our website.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to respond to your inquiries and provide you with legal services. We may also use your information to communicate with you about our services or to send you marketing materials if you have opted in to receive them. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the security of your information.

Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those websites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of those websites before providing any personal information.

Your Rights and Choices

You have certain rights and choices with respect to your personal information, such as the right to access, update, or delete your information, and the right to object to certain uses of your information. You may also choose not to receive marketing emails from us by following the unsubscribe link in the email. If you have any questions or concerns about your rights or choices, please contact us.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting a notice on our website or by sending you an email.
